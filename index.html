Dr. N K Chaudhry

Profile Summary

Dr. N K Chaudhry is a highly accomplished academic surgeon. He trained in General Medicine at Christian Medical College Ludhiana with great surgeons like Prof F C Eggleston and Dr. C M Singh. After passing his masters in 1988, Dr. Chaudhry got further trained in minimal access surgery at Kings College Hospital & Guys Hospital London in 1992 and is one of the pioneer laparoscopic surgeons in the region. Through a travelling fellowship of Indo American Cancer Association, he got trained in Hepatopancreatobiliary Surgery at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. He did Detroit Fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery at Wayne State University, Detroit.
